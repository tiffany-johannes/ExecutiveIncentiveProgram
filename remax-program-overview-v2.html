Why We Are Doing This

REMAX Executive's growth depends on bringing in productive agents and keeping the ones we have. Our current agents are the best source of quality recruits. This program gives them a compelling reason to help us grow while protecting our existing agent base through a retention layer.

Agents know agents

Our 420+ agents have relationships with hundreds of productive agents across the market. No recruiter reaches them like a trusted peer.

Quality over quantity

By tying rewards to recruit production volume we incentivize bringing in strong performers, not just warm bodies. More production = more tickets = better odds.

Revenue-positive by design

The program costs less than 3% of the revenue recruited agents generate. It pays for itself many times over from shared expenses alone.

Recognition and experiences win

Top producers earning $250K–$1M a year are not moved by small cash bonuses. Status, experiences, and public gala recognition create real excitement and real behavior change.

The core insight: A $1,000 cash bonus means little to an agent earning $500K a year. But a luxury trip drawing with their name called at the gala in front of peers, plus a meaningful marketing credit they can invest back into their own business — that creates genuine excitement and changes behavior.

What the Program Is

A three-layer incentive system covering individual agents, team leads, and offices — each designed differently, all working together toward one goal: growth and retention at REMAX Executive.

Layer 1 — Individual Agents

Recruit any agent to REMAX Executive. Earn raffle tickets based on their annual production volume. Earn a flexible marketing credit immediately. Compete in the prize drawing at the Annual Gala.

Layer 2 — Team Leads

Earn a flat retention reward when your team hits 80%, 90%, or 100% retention at REMAX Executive by December 31st. Choose between a swag bundle or marketing credit at each threshold.

Layer 3 — Offices

Offices compete on a net recruiting leaderboard updated quarterly. The top recruiting office wins a traveling trophy, an office celebration, and swag bundles for all staff.

How It Works

1
Individual Agent

Recruit an agent to REMAX Executive

Any REMAX Executive agent can refer a recruit. The recruit must join and begin producing under REMAX Executive. There is no cap on how many agents you can recruit.

2
Marketing Credit

Earn an immediate marketing credit based on their production tier

Tier 1 ($1.5M–$5M) earns you $500. Tier 2 ($5M–$10M) earns $1,000. Tier 3 ($10M+) earns $1,500. Spend on postcards, digital ads, swag, MasterPitch items, headshots, or any marketing spend — your choice.

3
Raffle Tickets

Accumulate tickets through the year based on your recruit's production

At year end your recruit's actual annual sales volume determines your ticket count — 1 ticket per $1M in production. A $6M producer earns you 6 tickets. Multiple recruits stack. The more productive your recruits, the better your odds.

4
Gala Drawing

All tickets go into one company-wide pool — drawn live at the Annual Gala

Every winner chooses their own prize from a curated menu in their tier — announced on stage in front of the entire company. The gala is the payoff, but the story builds all year through quarterly leaderboard updates.

5
Team Leads

Team leads earn flat retention rewards at year end — separate from the raffle

Team leads who hit 80%, 90%, or 100% retention of their REMAX team members by December 31st receive a flat reward — same amount regardless of team size. Protects margins while still creating a meaningful incentive.

Team Retention Layer

REMAX Executive has teams ranging from 2 to 50 agents. Team leads have enormous influence over whether agents stay at REMAX or leave. This layer creates a low-cost, margin-safe reward for keeping agents here — without impacting existing team agreements.

The risk without this layer: A 50-agent team with 20% annual turnover costs REMAX Executive $100,000+ in lost shared expenses alone. Team leads need to feel a real benefit from retention, not just from production.
